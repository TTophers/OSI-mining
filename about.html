About OSI Mining & Resources Inc.

Pioneering America's Resource Independence

OSI Mining & Resources Inc. is a Wyoming-incorporated critical minerals and precious metals company committed to advancing domestic supply, responsible extraction, and sustainable trade within the United States.

Our mission is to strengthen American resource independence through innovation, transparency, and ethical development.

From exploration to commercialization, OSI focuses on acquiring strategic mineral assets and metals, operating under rigorous U.S. compliance frameworks and guided by partnerships with national laboratories, universities, and private industry leaders.

We serve as a bridge between resource ownership, advanced technology, and capital markets, transforming natural potential into refined opportunity.

Our Vision

To become a U.S. leader in responsible mineral sourcing and advanced metals processing, driving a new era of sustainable industrial growth and technological independence.

Our Focus

  • Critical & Precious Metals: Gold, silver, platinum-group metals, and rare earth elements essential to clean-energy and defense industries.
  • Domestic Development: Prioritizing American sites and operations to reduce foreign dependency.
  • Technology Integration: Supporting innovation through collaborations and advanced separation systems.
  • Compliance & Ethics: Upholding the highest standards under FinCEN, BSA, OFAC, and EPA guidelines.

Our Commitment

OSI Mining & Resources stands for integrity, innovation, and impact. Every project we undertake— whether exploration, acquisition, or trading—is executed with transparency, environmental care, and a focus on long-term national benefit.

"At OSI, we believe American resources should power American innovation."

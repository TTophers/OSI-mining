Transforming electronic waste into valuable resources through innovative recycling processes

What is E-Waste?

Electronic waste, or e-waste, refers to discarded electrical or electronic devices. With the rapid advancement of , e-waste has become one of the fastest-growing waste streams globally, containing both valuable materials and hazardous substances.

At OSI Mineing Resources, we've developed cutting-edge processes to safely extract precious metals like gold, silver, and copper from e-waste, reducing environmental impact while recovering valuable resources.

Our 4-Step Recycling Process

1

Collection

Strategic collection points and partnerships ensure efficient e-waste gathering from various sources.

2

Sorting

Advanced sorting technologies separate materials by type and composition for optimal processing.

3

Extraction

Specialized extraction processes recover precious metals and valuable components from electronic devices.

4

Refining

High-purity refining techniques transform recovered materials into market-ready products and resources.

Samson Project (Arizona Division, Whickenburg, Arizona)

The Samson Project is OSI’s flagship U.S. gold and polymetallic development, located in the historic Whickenburg mining district. The project is advancing toward pilot-scale production with a focus on modern, environmentally responsible extraction methods and robust community engagement.

Current Status: Resource delineation underway; pilot plant commissioning planned for Q4 2024.

See extraction tech

Key Highlights

  • NI 43-101 compliant resource model in progress
  • Targeting gold, silver, and copper recovery
  • Dry-stack tailings and solar-assisted operations
  • Partnerships with local workforce and universities
  • Strategically located in a prolific U.S. mining region with established infrastructure and strong state support for sustainable development.
  • Resource expansion drilling ongoing
  • Community advisory board established
  • Permitting on track for 2024 pilot operations

Moonlight Project (Idaho Division – Salmon, Idaho)

Positioned to supply U.S. copper and critical minerals via next-generation refining, Moonlight is a cornerstone for OSI’s sustainable domestic production.

Status: Feasibility engineering in progress, with pilot-scale refining systems scheduled for deployment in 2025.

See refining systems

Key Highlights

  • The Moonlight Project is OSI’s advanced-stage copper and critical minerals site in the Idaho Division, near Salmon, Idaho.
  • Leverages modern refining systems and closed-loop processing to set new benchmarks for environmental responsibility and operational efficiency.
  • Low-carbon hydrometallurgical refining
  • On-site water recycling and tailings minimization
  • Critical minerals recovery (cobalt, rare earths)
  • Strong partnerships with regional stakeholders

Six Point Bull Elk Project (SPBE – Wyoming Division)

SPBE is OSI’s rare earth and battery materials pilot, located in Wyoming. The project is designed as a collaborative platform for advanced refining technologies, with a focus on maximizing domestic supply of strategic elements for clean energy and defense applications.

Status: Under review for exploration partnership and potential refining collaboration.

Explore refining collaboration

Key Highlights

  • U.S.-based rare earth pilot for strategic supply chains
  • Joint R&D with national labs and technology partners
  • Low-impact, modular refining systems
  • Supports EV, renewable, and defense sectors
